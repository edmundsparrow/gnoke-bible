✝️ Gnoke Bible

The complete King James Version — all 66 books, 1,189 chapters, and 31,102 verses — searchable and bookmarkable entirely in your browser.

Works completely offline after the first load. Your bookmarks never leave the device. No account. No server. No tracking.

⚙️ Under The Hood

Bible text King James Version (KJV) · Public domain
Data Inline JS array (kjv.js) · no fetch needed
Bookmarks localStorage · up to 100 verses
Search Full-text · all 31,102 verses
Network None required after first load
Stack HTML · CSS · Vanilla JS · PWA
